Frequently Asked Questions

What is the raffle about?

The raffle is part of our PVC recycling initiative to encourage public participation. By donating PVC, you get a chance to win exciting Target gift cards. The collection period runs until the end of October.

When is the raffle draw?

The raffle draw will take place at the end of October, once all donations are collected. Winners will be notified via email.

What are the prizes?

The prizes include $250, $150, and $100 Target gift cards. The more you donate, the higher your chances of winning!

How can I enter the raffle?

Simply fill out the raffle form with your details and upload a photo of your PVC donation. Every valid donation is an entry into the raffle.
