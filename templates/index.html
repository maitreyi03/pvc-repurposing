Our Purpose

What We Do

At PVSEEKERS, our mission is to engage with the community to reduce the impact of plastic pollution, specifically PVC plastics. We seek to repurpose PVC by collecting it from local residents, grinding it into small pellets, and reintegrating these pellets into PVC production. Our goal is to close the recycling loop, making a tangible difference in reducing plastic waste and promoting sustainability.

Important Research & Statistics

PVC's Impact on Environment

PVC is the third most widely produced synthetic plastic polymer. Improper disposal leads to harmful chemicals leaking into the soil and water, causing significant environmental damage.

Human Health Risks

The production and disposal of PVC release dangerous vinyl chloride, which has been linked to serious health risks, including cancer, liver damage, and respiratory issues.

Community Engagement

Recycling just 1 kg of PVC prevents approximately 2 kg of CO2 emissions, contributing to the fight against climate change and protecting our planet for future generations.
